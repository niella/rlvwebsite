What We Do

Our story starts around the run-up to the 2010 cloud boom, with two friends from different career backgrounds seeking a common platform for data backup and synchronisation. Having dabbled in various technologies and explored many avenues, we decided on a solid, reliable, cost-effective Linux toolset that we can implement and support through minimal effort. Over the years, numerous family, friends and associates have seen benefits from our implementations and still do. The success and the long term maintainability of years worth of tinkering and implementing brought us to the decision to bring these offerings to your doorstep.

Syncronised Backups

our business data is mostly in files. In fact, it's probably all there, on one licensed file server where you're not entirely sure who is accessing what, whether you'll get it back if it's lost, or how to recover from a disaster scenario. We want to show you just how simple and easy we can make this for you. We're so convinced that we will implement and maintain a backup solution for you for three months, synchronised off-site AND stored at a second DR location, free of charge!

Linux Support

Need the Linux savvy to maintain and service the infrastructure and solutions implemented in your data centre? No problem! We're passionate about the Linux world and the components we implement. We can fully support and assist the Linux platform of your choice or our recommendation from day one to day N. Knowledge transfer is another of our passions - let us train your IT and show you how to do it!

VPN Security

In these times of work-from-home, it is paramount that your VPN solution is secure and optimised. Rest easy with multi-layered encryption. Be assured of minimal footprint, low-impact connections that route only your server traffic and leaves day-to-day VOIP and conferencing to the employee ISP. IT organisations frequently overlook this and results in traffic congestion, impacts server availability, and causes poor user experience.

Infrastructure and Servers

Through the years, the auxiliary infrastructure has become somewhat of a passion of ours. Don't hesitate to reach out if you think we might be of assistance. We've implemented many of these and can probably contribute or value-add constructively.
